About This Community

Jamaat Abi Sibtayn is a Shia community grounded in love, loyalty, and khidmat. Our purpose is to preserve remembrance, uphold faith, and serve with sincerity and unity.

We strive to keep hearts connected to Karbala, to truth, and to spiritual responsibility — quietly, sincerely, and consistently.
